    Endgame

    Opposition

    Opposition is where 2 Kings are 2 squares apart on the same rank or file. Both kings prevent the each other's advance, creating a mutual blockade.

    The goal of opposition in the endgame is which King will get in front of the other King first. Whoever's King does, wins the position in the long run.

    Triangulation

    Triangulation is an idea that tries to put the opponent in zugzwang, a situation where no matter what the opponent does, it will be detreimental.

    Triangulation often requires high precision, counting, and calculation.

    See Image above for reference.


    Pawn Races

    A Pawn Race is an event where both side where are pushing their passed pawns to the end of the board, and whoever reaches the end first wins.

    Pawn races tend to be very nerve-racking as one can lose just because they were one tempo away, one square away from promotion.
