Resources

    Recommended Chess Resources:

    Chess.com

    Chess.com is the #1 platform for online chess. There are many free resources on chess.com, extraordinary features, and is an accessible platform to play with others.

    I've used chess.com for the longest and enjoy the formats of Puzzle Battle & Bullet Chess.

    I recommend chess.com for a way to practice chess and to be informed of the news in the chess world!


    Lichess.org

    Lichess.org is a free open-source Internet chess platform. There are many free resources on lichess.org, extraordinary features, and is an accessible platform to play with others.

    I personally use Lichess as my teaching platform. I would create lessons and teach my students from there.

    I recommend Lichess for a way to practice chess exercises, play tournaments, and watch top chess players play.


    Chess24.com

    Chess24.com is another amazing chess platform. There are many free resources on chess24.com, extraordinary features, and is an accessible platform to play with others.

    I use Chess24 as a way to view chess tournaments that are national, international, or local.

    I recommend Chess24 to learn from top chess players as they play in tournament with high money stakes.
