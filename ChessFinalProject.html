National Master Ralph Tan

Ralph Tan is currently a student at Saint Louis University (Class of '26). Majoring in Computer Science. Double Minioring in Finance & Engineering Math. Filipino-American, born in the United Arab Emirates, Abu Dhabi, and raised in Cleveland, Ohio. Ralph is a Shaker Heights Alumni. Ralph learned how to play chess at the age of 8 and played numerous tournaments since then. Ralph Tan has achieved the title National Master awarded by the United States Chess Federation. Ralph is currently on the road to chase after the Grandmaster title.
