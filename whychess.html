    Why Chess?

The Benefits of Chess

  • Problem Solving

    Chess provides a way for one to recognize similarities and patterns.

    It can allow one to create general strategies to solve certain problem(s).

    Such problems can include developing other choices or creativity.

  • Patience

    Patience is essential in Chess!

    The lack of patience in chess can lead to trouble such as Checkmate!

    Having patience in chess can sometimes turn a losing position into a winning position!

  • Sportsmanship

    Respect is important as it allows one to build feelings of trust, safety, and wellbeing.

    In Chess, everything is equal. From pieces to position, you and your opponent will treat each other as equals!

    It helps your mentality stay in uniform!
