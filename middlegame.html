    Middlegame

    Center Breakthrough

    A way to create action in the Middlegame is to breakthrough in the center. The center is the most important area of the board and where most of the action will be taking place.

    You can open up the center by advancing pieces in the center and force your opponent to react.

    In the case above, White pushed their pawn to e5 which attacks the Black pawn on d6 and Black knight on f6. White is looking to destroy Black's fortress.

    If the Black Knight of f6 moves away, then d6 pawn will fall. If Black captures the pawn on e5, then the Knight captures back putting pressure on the Black Bishop on d7.


    Pawn Storms

    Another way to create action in the Middlegame to start a Pawn Storm. A Pawn Storm is a movement of several pawns towards the opponent's defense, the area where the opponent's King is.

    This Middlegame idea can lead to rapid success as it causes the opponent to panic and react, typically in a poor way.


    Rook Lifts

    A Rook Lift is a manuever that activates the Rook through the process of moving it up, to the side, and in front of its own pawns.

    A powerful Middlegame idea that is effective and efficient as it can quickly create threats.
