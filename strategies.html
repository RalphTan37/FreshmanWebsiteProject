    Strategies

    Rooks on the 7th Rank

    Placing a Rook on the 7th rank is a very powerful strategy as it threatens the opponent's unadavanced pawns and can cut the opponent's King from moving to the 7th rank.

    Rooks on the 7th Rank are sometimes called "Pigs on the Seventh" because the Rook will eat all the pawns on the 7th rank like a pig!

    Rooks on Open Files

    Having a Rook on an open file is a very powerful strategy as it allows the Rook to easily infiltrate the opponent's territory and attack!

    Batteries

    Creating Batteries is a very powerful strategy as it allows 2 or more pieces to control a file, rank, or diagonally.
